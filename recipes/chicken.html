Italian Chicken Marinade Recipe

A lovely looking chicken marinade

This recipe will go over how to make Italian Chicken Marinade.

Marinades like this one trace their history back to ancient Egyptian times.

Ingrediants

  • 1 bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Instructions

  1. Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together and add chicken.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess.
  4. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center.
