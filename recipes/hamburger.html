Hamburger Recipe

An amazing looking burger

In the recipe we will learn how to make an American classic.

The hamburger is a staple in American cuisine

Ingrediants

  • 1.5 lbss ground beef
  • .5 chopped onion
  • .5 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 oz dry onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil
  2. Combine ingrediants into 4 patties
  3. Cook patties until no longer pink in the center, about 4 to 5 minutes each side
